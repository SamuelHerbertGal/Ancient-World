THE ANCIENT WORLD

You step into the Verdant Preserve — an ancient magical sanctuary now torn by a demonic rift. Hidden deep within its ruined temple lies the legendary Sunstone. Seek the wizard, gather the moonflowers, cut down the shadow demons, free the Forest Guardian, kindle the ancient braziers, and cast down the Demon Champion and the Demon Lord to claim the artifact.
