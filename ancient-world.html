THE ANCIENT WORLD

You step into the Verdant Preserve — an ancient sanctuary now overrun by monsters of Greek legend: harpies, cyclopes, gorgons, a minotaur, and worse. Deep in its ruined temple lies the legendary Sunstone. Seek out three mages — Eldrin, the oracle Pythia, and the enchantress Circe — gather the moonflowers and golden relics, and cast down Medusa the Gorgon Queen and Cerberus, Hound of Hades to claim the artifact.
